## Overview

The main challenge facing Indonesian tourism is not a lack of destinations, but rather an uneven distribution of tourists and a travel experience that remains fragmented and unoptimized. Virtopia is an innovative travel platform that integrates artificial intelligence and metaverse technology to transform how travelers plan and explore their trips. Designed as a next-generation smart tourism ecosystem, Virtopia addresses modern challenges such as inaccurate destination information, limited exposure for local destinations, and the difficulty travelers face in picturing a place before actually visiting it. This was a group project developed for a design competition.

## Key Features

- **AI Trip Planner**: An automated itinerary planning feature that generates fast, personalized, and efficient travel plans.
- **Metaverse-Based Virtual Exploration**: A "try before you travel" experience that lets travelers immersively explore a destination, view travel routes, observe environmental conditions, and gauge crowd levels before deciding to visit, built on a Roblox-based visualization.
- **Integrated Booking System**: A booking system designed to be efficient and personalized, while also considering the needs of local SMEs and rural tourism villages.
- **Local Destination Promotion**: A platform for local tourism SMEs, rural tourism villages, and hidden-gem destinations to professionally promote themselves through metaverse-based visualization.

## My Role and Contributions

I served as the Lead UI/UX Designer on this project. While the platform concept was developed as a group, I was responsible for the overall UI/UX design, contributing the majority of the design work, including the complete design system and an interactive Figma prototype. I also created the user flow that maps out the end-to-end experience, from itinerary planning through virtual exploration to booking.

## Challenges and Learnings

Working under the constraints of a design competition trained me to simplify complex concepts, such as combining AI planning with metaverse exploration, into flows that can be understood quickly by both judges and potential users, without sacrificing the depth of the platform's core ideas.

## Outcome

Virtopia was delivered as a complete interactive Figma prototype, featuring a full design system and a coherent user journey that demonstrates how AI and metaverse technology can work together to support a more balanced, sustainable, and immersive smart tourism experience in Indonesia.
